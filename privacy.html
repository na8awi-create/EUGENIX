Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

Magic Pour Over does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Magic Pour Over ("the App"), developed by Eugenix.lab, handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Magic Pour Over does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Brewing data or history (stored locally on device only)
  • Bluetooth device information

3. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the App (such as brew history and recipe preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party. You have full control over this data and can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. Bluetooth (BLE) Usage

The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to compatible smart scales. This connection is direct between your device and the scale — no data is transmitted to external servers. We do not store or share any information about connected Bluetooth devices.

5. Third-Party Services

Magic Pour Over does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
